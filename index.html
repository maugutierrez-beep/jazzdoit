FAQ

Quick answers

Is it really completely free? +
Yes. jazzdoit is completely free — unlimited tasks, unlimited projects, all features included. No credit card, no hidden limits, no "premium" tier. We may introduce optional paid features in the future, but your current usage will never be cut off.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. Each account is completely isolated — only you can see your tasks. We use row-level security (RLS) so even our own database queries can't read your data without your session. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
Does it work without internet? +
Yes. jazzdoit is a PWA (Progressive Web App) that works offline. Your tasks are saved locally and sync automatically when you reconnect. Install it on your phone and it works like a native app — no internet required to view or edit your tasks.
Do I need to download anything? +
No download needed to use jazzdoit — it runs in any browser. But you can install it on your phone like a native app: on iPhone, tap Share → Add to Home Screen. On Android, tap the install icon in Chrome. It's instant and takes up almost no space.
