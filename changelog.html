What's new in jazzdoit

We ship improvements every week. Here's what's changed recently.

Jun 27, 2026
v0.9 — Major Update

Bulk actions, dark mode & advanced filters

Our biggest update yet. Select multiple tasks at once, filter your list by project or person, and switch to dark mode for those late-night planning sessions.

  • Bulk actions — select multiple tasks, then complete, reschedule, move or delete them all at once
  • Dark mode — automatic (follows your device) or manual toggle with ⌨ Shift+D
  • Advanced filters — filter by project, person, status (overdue/today/no date) or flagged
  • Task time — add a specific time to any task, with push notification 10 min before
  • Subtasks from My Day — check off subtasks directly from the day view without opening the task
  • Keyboard shortcuts — press ? to see all shortcuts. N for new task, / to search, G+D for My Day
  • Invite colleagues — share jazzdoit with a personalized invite link from your profile
New Improvement
Jun 25, 2026
v0.8

Mobile-first overhaul

A complete audit and fix of the mobile experience — plus new features requested by early users.

  • + New task button always visible in the top bar on mobile — no more digging through the menu
  • Task modal now opens as a bottom sheet on mobile with autofocus on the title field
  • Password reset — forgot your password? Now you can recover it via email
  • Task templates — 4 pre-built sets (weekly review, project kickoff, client follow-up, daily routine)
  • Mi Progreso / My Progress view — streak, completion rate, top projects, export buttons
  • WhatsApp tooltip — suggests sending a follow-up when you assign a task to someone
  • Offline indicator — yellow banner when disconnected, sync animation when reconnected
New Fix
Jun 20, 2026
v0.7

Share tasks, streaks & export

New ways to share your work and see your progress over time.

  • Share any task via a public link — recipient sees the task read-only, no account required
  • Streak tracking — consecutive days active, with celebrations at 3, 7, 14 and 30 days
  • Export your data in CSV or JSON format from the Progress view
  • Push notifications — get reminded when tasks are due or overdue
  • Onboarding tour for new users — 3-step guide to the key features
  • Better empty states with clear call-to-action buttons

Jun 12, 2026
v0.6 — Beta Launch

jazzdoit goes public

The first public beta of jazzdoit — a task manager built for people who manage others, not just themselves.

  • My Day view — today and tomorrow side by side
  • WhatsApp follow-up — generate a message with all pending tasks for any person in one tap
  • Daily email — your agenda delivered to your inbox every morning
  • Google login and email/password signup
  • Projects with custom colors
  • Recurring tasks — daily, weekly, monthly
  • Works offline as a PWA — install it on your phone like a native app
  • English and Spanish (auto-detected)
